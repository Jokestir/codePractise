for loop
========

* for loops iterates for a fixed number of times

for iterating_var in sequence:
	do_something()



* range(i,j) returns a integer sequence from i to j-1

or range(i,j,step)


e.g: 

1) for letter in 'Python'
	print(letter)

  outputs to each letter of Python

2) for fruits in ['banana','apple','mango']
	print(fruits)

 outputs banana apple and mango

3) for integer in range(10,20)
	print(str(integer)) 

	outputs prints integers from 10 to 20 (excluding 20)

4) for integer in range(10)
	print (integer) 
	
	outputs prints from 0 to 9
